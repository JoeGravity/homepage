I provide highly skilled, experienced graphic design services from concept to completion. I've made logos, brochures, catalogs, packaging and print ads. I am available fulltime, parttime, on- or off-site for projects. Give me a call.

Design Process
Here's a very general outline of my design process. (This can change depending on the project, and is often abbreviated for minor updates, simple projects, rough prestentations, etc.)

When I set out to design a print item for you, I usually meet with you first to discuss your needs and options, and to work out what text, photos and artwork you'll provide and what you'll want from me. Then I provide you with some rough options on screen or paper. Once we determine the general direction of the piece's appearance and function, I can begin pulling the content together and building the items, sending you updates in printouts or pdf email format. When you approve the finished piece, I can send the finished product to you or your print vendor. Typically, you'll want electronic files via e-mail, CD-ROM or web server.

Rates
I try to keep my rates reasonable, and I can be flexible as needed. I can charge per hour, per-project, or a combination arrangement such as a capped rate. Give me a call.
