I've made illustrations in both digital and various traditional media, including pencil, marker, gouache paint and combinations of all the above.

Artwork Process
Here's a very general outline of my illustration process. (This can change depending on the project, and is often abbreviated for minor updates, simple projects, rough presentations, etc.)

When I set out to make a custom illustration for you, I usually meet with you first to discuss your needs and options, and to work out what media and style of artwork you'll want from me. Then I provide you with some rough options on screen or paper. Once we determine the general direction of the piece's appearance and function, I can begin creating the piece, sending you updates in printouts or pdf email format. When you approve the finished piece, I can send the finished product to you or your print vendor. Typically, you'll want electronic files via e-mail, CD-ROM or web server.

Rates
I try to keep my rates reasonable, and I can be flexible as needed. I can charge per hour, per-project, or a combination arrangement such as a capped rate. Give me a call.
