Much of my professional work has been preparing of print items for press, from checking typography to scanning to film output to traditional pasteup (weren't those the days). I can do your chart formatting, digital manipulation, whatever. If you need, say, a large, complex catalog fleshed out from a rough layout and a list of products, I'm your man.

Artwork Process
Here's a very general outline of my production process. (This can change depending on the project, and is often abbreviated for minor updates, simple projects, rough presentations, etc.)

For preparing a project for press, I usually talk with you first to discuss your needs and options, and work out scheduling and logistics. As I make progress on the piece, I send you updates in printouts or pdf email format. When you approve the finished piece, I can send the finished product to you or your print vendor. Typically, you'll want electronic files via e-mail, CD-ROM or web server.

Rates
I try to keep my rates reasonable, and I can be flexible as needed. I can charge per hour, per-project, or a combination arrangement such as a capped rate. Give me a call.
