I've made several websites for different clients. I usually build them using Dreamweaver on a Mac, along with image-preparation software such as Photoshop and Fireworks.

Design Process
When I set out to design a website for you, we discuss your needs and options, and work out what text, photos, music and artwork you'll provide and what you'll want from me.

Then I provide you with rough options on screen or paper. Once we determine the general direction of the site's appearance and function, I can begin pulling the content together and building the site, sending you updates and partial builds along the way for feedback. When the preliminary site is built, I can post it on a temporary site for proofing and debugging.

When you approve the finished site, I can then upload it to a web server and you're online. I can further submit your web address to various search engines and post updates as needed. Alternately, you may want to manage your own updates, etc. once it's online--I can help you establish that, too.

Rates
I try to keep my rates reasonable, and I can be flexible as needed. I can charge per hour, per-project, or a combination arrangement such as a capped rate. Give me a call.
